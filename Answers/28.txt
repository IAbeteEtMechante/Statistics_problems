for an exponential distribution, the first moment is:
E(X) = 1/theta

for the sample of size 6:

the mean is 3.66

by equating the sample mean and the population mean, we get:
1/theta = 3.66

so theta = 0.27


##########Python code########################
>>> aa = [11.37, 3, 0.15, 4.27, 2.56, 0.59]
>>> sum(aa)/len(aa)
3.6566666666666663
>>> 1/sum(aa)*len(aa)
0.27347310847766637
